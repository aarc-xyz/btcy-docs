## Overview

Unwrapping converts your BTCY back to iBTCY at a 1:1 ratio. This is required before redeeming to BTC.

---

## Prerequisites

To unwrap BTCY:

1. Hold BTCY in your wallet
2. Your wallet must be allowlisted (to receive iBTCY)
3. Have ETH for gas

<Warning>
**Allowlist required.** Only allowlisted addresses can receive iBTCY. If your wallet is not allowlisted, you cannot unwrap.
</Warning>

---

## Unwrapping Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Allowlist">
    Confirm your wallet is on the allowlist. If not, contact us to begin onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Call the withdraw function on the BTCY vault.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive iBTCY">
    iBTCY tokens are transferred to your wallet 1:1.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Key Details

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Exchange rate** | 1:1 (always) |
| **Fee** | No fee |
| **Speed** | Instant (single transaction) |
| **Requirement** | Wallet must be allowlisted |

---

## After Unwrapping

With iBTCY, you can:

- **Redeem** — Convert to BTC via Sygnum
- **Hold** — Continue earning yield
- **Wrap again** — Convert back to BTCY if needed
- **Transfer** — Send to other allowlisted addresses

---

## Not Allowlisted?

If your wallet is not allowlisted, you have two options:

1. **Get allowlisted** — Complete KYC and onboarding
2. **Sell BTCY** — Trade on secondary markets (no redemption)
