## Overview

Wrapping converts your iBTCY into BTCY tokens at a 1:1 ratio, enabling DeFi composability.

---

## Prerequisites

To wrap iBTCY:

1. Hold iBTCY in your wallet
2. Your wallet must be allowlisted for iBTCY transfers
3. Have ETH for gas

---

## Wrapping Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Approve">
    Approve the BTCY vault to spend your iBTCY tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Call the deposit function with the amount of iBTCY to wrap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive BTCY">
    BTCY tokens are minted to your wallet 1:1.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Key Details

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Exchange rate** | 1:1 (always) |
| **Fee** | No fee |
| **Speed** | Instant (single transaction) |
| **Reversible** | Yes, via unwrapping |

---

## After Wrapping

Once you have BTCY, you can:

- **Hold** — Value accrues from underlying fund
- **Transfer** — Send to any address (no restrictions)
- **Trade** — Swap on DEXs
- **Collateralize** — Use in lending protocols
- **LP** — Provide liquidity

<Info>
BTCY can be held by anyone. The allowlist restriction only applies to iBTCY.
</Info>

---

## Unwrapping

To convert BTCY back to iBTCY (required for redemption to BTC), see [Unwrapping](/btcy/unwrapping).
