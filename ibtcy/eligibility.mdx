# Eligibility Requirements

iBTCY is an **institutional product** designed for qualified investors. This page outlines who can participate.

## Who Can Invest?

iBTCY is available to:

- Institutional investors
- Accredited investors
- High-net-worth individuals meeting local requirements

<Info>
  Minimum investment: **$100,000 USD** equivalent in BTC
</Info>

## KYC Requirements

All investors must complete Know Your Customer (KYC) verification:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identity Verification">
    Government-issued ID, proof of address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accreditation Check">
    Verification of accredited/qualified investor status
  </Step>
  <Step title="AML Screening">
    Anti-money laundering and sanctions screening
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allowlist Addition">
    Once approved, your wallet address is added to the allowlist
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Jurisdiction Restrictions

The following jurisdictions are **prohibited** from participating:

<Warning>
  Persons residing in or citizens of the following jurisdictions cannot subscribe, acquire, or redeem iBTCY:
</Warning>

- United States of America
- Afghanistan
- Belarus
- Central African Republic
- Cuba
- Democratic Republic of Congo
- Iran
- Iraq
- Libya
- Mali
- Myanmar (Burma)
- Nicaragua
- North Korea
- Russia
- Somalia
- South Sudan
- Sudan
- Syria
- Venezuela
- Yemen
- Zimbabwe

*This list may be updated to reflect changes in regulations or sanctions.*

## Entity Requirements

For institutional investors:

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Legal entity documentation | Certificate of incorporation, articles |
| Beneficial ownership | UBO identification for >25% owners |
| Authorized signatories | Documentation of signing authority |
| Source of funds | Declaration of fund source |

## Ongoing Compliance

Once onboarded, investors must:

1. **Maintain eligibility**: Report any changes in status
2. **Remain compliant**: Not engage in prohibited activities
3. **Update KYC**: Periodic re-verification may be required

<Note>
  Failure to maintain compliance may result in blocklisting and restricted access to your tokens.
</Note>
