## Jurisdiction Restrictions

iBTCY is **not available** in:

- United States (US persons)
- Sanctioned jurisdictions (OFAC, EU, UN sanctions lists)

Other jurisdictions may have specific requirements. Contact us to confirm eligibility.

---

## KYC Requirements

All investors must complete Know Your Customer (KYC) verification:

1. **Identity verification** — Government-issued ID, proof of address
2. **Entity verification** — For institutions: incorporation documents, beneficial ownership
3. **Investor qualification** — Proof of accredited/professional investor status
4. **Source of funds** — AML compliance documentation
