## Management Fees

The underlying fund charges management fees that are reflected in the NAV. These fees cover:

- Custody and administration
- Yield strategy execution
- Operational costs

<Info>
Management fees are already accounted for in the NAV. The yield you see is net of fees.
</Info>

---

## Gas Costs

All on-chain operations require gas:

- Wrap/unwrap operations
- Token transfers
- Redemption requests

Gas costs depend on network conditions and are paid by the user.
