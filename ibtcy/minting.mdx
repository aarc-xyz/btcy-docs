## Overview

Minting iBTCY converts your BTC into yield-bearing iBTCY tokens at the current NAV price.

---

## Prerequisites

Before minting, you must:

1. Complete KYC verification
2. Sign subscription documents
3. Have an allowlisted wallet address

## Minting Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit BTC">
    Send BTC to the designated Sygnum custody address provided during onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Confirmation">
    Your deposit is confirmed and a mint request is created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="NAV Calculation">
    The current NAV is determined based on the fund's assets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="iBTCY Minted">
    iBTCY tokens are minted to your allowlisted wallet address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Timing

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| BTC confirmation | ~1 hour (6 confirmations) |
| Mint processing | Same business day |
| iBTCY delivery | Immediate after processing |

<Info>
Minting uses the NAV at the time of processing, not the time of deposit.
</Info>

## After Minting

Once you have iBTCY, you can:

- **Hold** — Earn yield from the underlying fund
- **Wrap to BTCY** — Convert to BTCY for DeFi use
- **Transfer** — Send to other allowlisted addresses
- **Redeem** — Convert back to BTC
