## Overview

Redeeming iBTCY converts your tokens back to BTC at the current NAV price.

---

## Prerequisites

To redeem iBTCY:

1. Hold iBTCY in your allowlisted wallet
2. If you have BTCY, unwrap it first (see [Unwrapping](/btcy/unwrapping))

---

## Redemption Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Initiate a redemption request specifying the amount of iBTCY to redeem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Validation">
    The request is validated against your balance and eligibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="NAV Calculation">
    The redemption amount is calculated at the current NAV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="BTC Transfer">
    BTC is transferred to your designated address after processing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Timing

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Request submission | Immediate |
| Processing | 1-3 business days |
| BTC delivery | Same day as processing |

<Note>
Redemption timing depends on fund liquidity and operational windows.
</Note>

---

## Redemption Fees

---

## Important Notes

- Redemptions are processed in the order received
- Large redemptions may be processed over multiple days
- NAV at time of processing determines the redemption amount
