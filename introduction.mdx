BTCY is a tokenized Bitcoin yield product backed by **Sygnum Bank**, a Swiss-regulated digital asset bank. 

It bridges institutional-grade BTC yield strategies with DeFi composability through a two-token architecture.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="iBTCY" icon="building-columns">
    **Institutional token.** NAV-based pricing. Restricted to qualified investors. Backed by Sygnum's actively managed BTC yield fund.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BTCY" icon="coins">
    **DeFi wrapper.** ERC-4626 vault. 1:1 backed by iBTCY. Composable with lending protocols, DEXs, and collateral markets.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why BTCY

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bitcoin yield without selling BTC" icon="bitcoin">
    Earn 5-6% APY on your Bitcoin exposure through Sygnum's institutional yield strategies. No need to sell or swap your BTC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Swiss-regulated custody" icon="shield-check">
    Assets held by Sygnum Bank, a FINMA-licensed Swiss digital asset bank. Bank-grade security and regulatory clarity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="DeFi composable" icon="puzzle-piece">
    BTCY works like any ERC-20. Use it as collateral, provide liquidity, or integrate into any DeFi protocol.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multi-chain" icon="link">
    Available on Ethereum, BNB Chain, Avalanche, and Base.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
