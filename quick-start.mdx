## Step 1: Verify Eligibility

BTCY is available to qualified investors. This includes:

- Institutional investors (funds, treasuries, DAOs)
- Accredited investors / High-net-worth individuals
- Professional investors (as defined by applicable regulations)

## Step 2: Complete Onboarding

Contact our team to begin the onboarding process:

1. **Submit inquiry** — Share your organization details
2. **Complete KYC** — Verify your identity and investor status
3. **Sign agreements** — Review and sign the subscription documents
4. **Fund account** — Deposit BTC to Sygnum custody

## Step 3: Mint & Use

Once onboarded:

1. **Mint iBTCY** — Your BTC is converted to iBTCY at the current NAV
2. **Wrap to BTCY** — Deposit iBTCY to receive BTCY (optional, for DeFi use)
3. **Earn yield** — Your position accrues yield from the underlying fund
