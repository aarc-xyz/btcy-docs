## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is BTCY?">
    BTCY is a tokenized Bitcoin yield product backed by Sygnum Bank. It uses a two-token system: iBTCY (institutional, NAV-based) and BTCY (DeFi-composable wrapper).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What yield can I expect?">
    The target yield is 5-6% APY, generated by Sygnum's actively managed BTC yield strategies. Actual yield may vary.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Who can invest?">

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is this available in the US?">
    No. BTCY is not available to US persons or residents.
  </Accordion>

## Operations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    Contact us to begin the onboarding process. You'll complete KYC, sign documents, and deposit BTC. See [Quick Start](/quick-start).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does onboarding take?">
    Typically 3-5 business days from initial contact to first mint.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the minimum investment?">

  </Accordion>

    Unwrap BTCY to iBTCY (if applicable), then submit a redemption request. BTC is delivered after processing. See [Redeeming](/ibtcy/redeeming).

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What networks is BTCY on?">
    Ethereum, BNB Chain, Avalanche, and Base. See [Supported Networks](/resources/supported-networks).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between iBTCY and BTCY?">
    iBTCY is the institutional token with transfer restrictions (allowlist). BTCY is the unrestricted wrapper for DeFi use. They're always exchangeable 1:1.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are the contracts audited?">
    Audits are in progress. See [Audits](/trust-security/audits).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is the code open source?">
    Yes, all contracts will be verified and open source on block explorers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who holds the BTC?">
    Sygnum Bank, a FINMA-regulated Swiss digital asset bank. See [Custody](/trust-security/custody).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if Sygnum fails?">
    Client assets are segregated from bank assets and protected under Swiss banking law.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if the smart contracts have a bug?">
    Contracts are audited and upgradeable via timelocked UUPS proxies. Emergency pause is available.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
