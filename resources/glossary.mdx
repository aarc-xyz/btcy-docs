## A

**Allowlist**
A list of addresses permitted to hold or transfer iBTCY. Required for compliance with securities regulations.

**AMC (Actively Managed Certificate)**
A Swiss-regulated investment structure used by iBTCY. Provides clear legal framework for tokenized securities.

## B

**BTCY**
The DeFi-composable wrapper token. An ERC-4626 vault that wraps iBTCY 1:1.

**Blocklist**
A list of addresses prohibited from interacting with the protocol. Used for sanctions compliance.

## E

**ERC-4626**
Ethereum standard for tokenized vaults. Defines a standard API for yield-bearing vaults.

**ERC-20**
Ethereum standard for fungible tokens. Both iBTCY and BTCY are ERC-20 compatible.

## I

**iBTCY**
The institutional token representing ownership in Sygnum's BTC yield fund. NAV-based pricing, restricted transfers.

## K

**KYC (Know Your Customer)**
Identity verification process required to hold iBTCY. Ensures compliance with AML regulations.

## N

**NAV (Net Asset Value)**
The value of the underlying fund's assets minus liabilities. iBTCY price is based on NAV.

## O

**Oracle**
External data source that provides on-chain price data. BTCY uses Chainlink oracles for NAV pricing.

## Q

**Qualified Investor**
An investor meeting certain financial thresholds or professional status. Required to hold iBTCY.

## S

**Sygnum Bank**
Swiss-regulated digital asset bank providing custody and fund management for BTCY.

## U

**UUPS (Universal Upgradeable Proxy Standard)**
Upgrade pattern used by BTCY contracts. Allows bug fixes while maintaining security through timelocks.

## W

**Wrap**
Converting iBTCY to BTCY by depositing into the vault.

**Unwrap**
Converting BTCY back to iBTCY by withdrawing from the vault.
