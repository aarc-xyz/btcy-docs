## Network Details

### Ethereum

The primary network for BTCY. Highest security and liquidity.

- **Block time:** ~12 seconds
- **Finality:** ~15 minutes
- **Gas:** Variable

### BNB Chain

Lower gas costs, high throughput.

- **Block time:** ~3 seconds
- **Finality:** ~45 seconds
- **Gas:** Low

### Avalanche C-Chain

Fast finality, EVM compatible.

- **Block time:** ~2 seconds
- **Finality:** ~1 second
- **Gas:** Low

### Base

Coinbase L2, low cost Ethereum security.

- **Block time:** ~2 seconds
- **Finality:** Inherits Ethereum
- **Gas:** Very low
