# Risk Factors

<Warning>
  Investing in BTCY involves risks. Please read this section carefully before participating.
</Warning>

## Smart Contract Risk

BTCY relies on smart contracts for token management and operations.

**Risks include:**
- Bugs or vulnerabilities in contract code
- Unexpected behavior in edge cases
- Upgrade mechanism exploitation

**Mitigations:**
- Multiple independent audits
- Timelocked upgrades (48h normal, 12h emergency)
- Circuit breakers for large operations
- Formal verification of critical paths

## Custodial Risk

BTC backing iBTCY is held by third-party custodians.

**Risks include:**
- Custodian insolvency
- Unauthorized access to funds
- Operational failures

**Mitigations:**
- Sygnum Bank (Swiss-regulated) as primary custodian
- Segregated accounts
- Insurance coverage
- Regular attestations

## Oracle Risk

NAV pricing depends on Chainlink oracle feeds.

**Risks include:**
- Oracle manipulation
- Stale or incorrect prices
- Oracle downtime

**Mitigations:**
- Multiple Chainlink safety checks
- Price bounds (0.5-2.0 BTC range)
- Maximum 5% deviation per update
- Staleness handling (24h warning, 48h block)

## Market Risk

**Risks include:**
- BTC price volatility
- NAV fluctuations
- Liquidity constraints during market stress

**Mitigations:**
- Sleeve reserve (~32% liquid)
- Instant + monthly redemption paths
- No leverage in underlying strategy

## Regulatory Risk

**Risks include:**
- Changes in regulatory frameworks
- Increased compliance requirements
- Jurisdiction-specific restrictions

**Mitigations:**
- Swiss AMC structure
- Licensed custodians (FINMA, BMA)
- Proactive compliance monitoring
- Allowlist/blocklist controls

## Liquidity Risk

**Risks include:**
- Insufficient sleeve for instant redemptions
- Monthly dealing delays
- DeFi liquidity constraints for BTCY

**Mitigations:**
- ~32% AUM in liquid sleeve
- Partial processing capability
- Multiple redemption paths

## Counterparty Risk

**Risks include:**
- Sygnum fund performance
- Archax execution issues
- Third-party service failures

**Mitigations:**
- Established institutional partners
- Diversified service providers
- Operational redundancy

## Execution Risk

**Risks include:**
- Transaction delays
- Network congestion
- Failed transactions

**Mitigations:**
- Ethereum mainnet reliability
- Gas fee estimation
- Retry mechanisms in backend

## Operational Risk

**Risks include:**
- Key management failures
- Multisig coordination issues
- Backend system downtime

**Mitigations:**
- Cold 3/5 multisig for critical operations
- Ops 2/3 multisig for daily operations
- Redundant infrastructure

## Blocklist/Freeze Risk

**Risks include:**
- Legitimate users incorrectly blocklisted
- Tokens frozen without recourse

**Mitigations:**
- Clear compliance procedures
- Appeals process
- Force functions require blocklist first (audit trail)

## Technology Risk

**Risks include:**
- Ethereum network failures
- Hard fork complications
- Upgradeability issues

**Mitigations:**
- Battle-tested Ethereum infrastructure
- Conservative upgrade practices
- Emergency pause capabilities
