## Smart Contract Risk

BTCY relies on smart contracts that may contain bugs or vulnerabilities.

**Mitigations:**
- Independent security audits
- Timelocked upgrades
- Emergency pause functionality
- Open source code

---

## Custody Risk

Assets are held by third-party custodians (Sygnum Bank).

**Mitigations:**
- FINMA-regulated Swiss bank
- Segregated client assets
- Insurance coverage
- Multi-signature controls

---

## Market Risk

The value of iBTCY and BTCY depends on the underlying BTC and fund performance.

**Considerations:**
- BTC price volatility affects NAV
- Yield is not guaranteed
- Past performance does not indicate future results

---

## Oracle Risk

NAV pricing depends on Chainlink oracles.

**Mitigations:**
- Staleness checks reject outdated data
- Price bounds flag extreme values
- Multiple data sources
- Fallback mechanisms

---

## Liquidity Risk

Redemptions depend on fund liquidity and operational capacity.

**Considerations:**
- Large redemptions may be delayed
- NAV at processing time determines redemption value
- No guaranteed redemption timeline

---

## Regulatory Risk

Regulations around digital assets and tokenized securities continue to evolve.

**Considerations:**
- Regulatory changes may affect availability
- Jurisdiction restrictions may change
- Tax treatment varies by jurisdiction

---

## Counterparty Risk

BTCY involves multiple counterparties:

| Counterparty | Risk |
|--------------|------|
| Sygnum Bank | Custody and fund management |
| Archax | Execution services |
| Chainlink | Oracle data |
| Aarc | Smart contract deployment |

---

## Technology Risk

Blockchain infrastructure may experience:

- Network congestion
- Hard forks
- Protocol changes
- Validator issues
