# Custody & Reserves

BTCY is backed by real Bitcoin held in institutional custody.

## Custody Partners

### Sygnum Bank

- **Type**: Swiss-regulated bank
- **License**: FINMA banking license
- **Role**: Custodian for the underlying BTC yield fund

### Archax

- **Type**: UK FCA-regulated exchange
- **Role**: Execution and custody partner

## Reserve Structure

The underlying fund maintains reserves in two components:

| Component | Allocation | Purpose |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Sleeve** | ~32% of AUM | Liquid BTC for instant redemptions |
| **Yield Strategies** | ~68% of AUM | Generating ~5% APY |

<Info>
  The sleeve ensures users can redeem instantly (T+0/T+1) without waiting for monthly dealing windows.
</Info>

## NAV Calculation

Net Asset Value is calculated as:

```
NAV = Total Fund Assets (in BTC) / Total iBTCY Supply
```

- Updated via Chainlink oracle
- Reflects yield accumulation over time
- Starting NAV: 1.0 BTC per iBTCY
